Frequently Asked Questions
How secure is my data?

All files are encrypted with AES-256 both at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Your data is stored redundantly across multiple data centers.

Can I access my files offline?

Yes! Our desktop and mobile apps let you mark files and folders for offline access. They'll sync automatically when you're back online.

What file types can I store?

Anything! Documents, photos, videos, code, archives‚Äîwe support all file types. We also provide previews for common formats like PDFs, images, and Office documents.

How does team billing work?

Team plans are billed per user per month. You can add or remove users anytime and your bill adjusts automatically. Annual plans get 2 months free.

Can I migrate from another service?

Absolutely. We offer free migration from Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, and Box. Our team will help transfer your files and folder structure.
